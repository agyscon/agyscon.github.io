Project Information

Wildfires, massive storms, global heatwaves: Feeling helpless and overwhelmed about climate change? Hazel provides a refuge for you to free yourself from climate-change worries. With Hazel, you contribute to support projects that lower global carbon levels, earn points and badges for your role and see how you stack up against others in the community while having fun too. Here’s how it works: Sign up and choose a carbon removal project to support, such as saving a riverfront in a rainforest. Can’t pick? Choose them all! Hazel lets you support a range of bespoke projects with a single tap. What kind of positive impact do you want to bring into the world? Simply choose to eliminate the environmental cost of a tank of gas, an upcoming flight or your monthly electrical use. Or, make a flat contribution equal to the amount of carbon we all need to remove each month to reverse climate change today. Share with your friends to multiply the positive climate change impact.
