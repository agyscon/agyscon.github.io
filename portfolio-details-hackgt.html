HackGT Project

  • Technologies: Unity
  • Project date: Fall 2019

Project Information

A Hackathon project where students from across the world come together for a weekend in the fall to create innovative projects. My team built an application that rewards players for not looking at their phone. After "focusing", they are rewarded with an animal in their terrarium. I took the time to train and lead a team of developers who had never learned Unity before.
